Hi, I am Di Sun, an impact-driven designer focused on creating simple and elegant solutions for complex problems. Currently, I am working as a product designer for Google based in San Francisco CA. I majored in photography and transitioned my career to be a designer about 2 years ago. Since then, I've been very fortunate enough to work on really cool projects . When I am not designing, I'm usually visiting different art museums and trying out different restaurants.

Every designer and organization has different needs, and therefore different processes. My design process is based on Lean UX , which emphasizes rapid, iterative UX phases, placing a heavy focus on team collaboration , user input, and measurable results. Functions overlap, allowing designers and developers to work together from the very start. This process is quick, efficient, accurate, precise, and adaptive. It is custom-tailored to each project, allowing it to yield high quality UX in any circumstance, when applied correctly.

If you want to know more about my UX process, I constantly write and update my design process page here. If you made this far, let's have coffee together to talk about design, art and delicious food. Please feel free to shoot me an email: sundi73@gmail.com or connect me on LinkedIn.
